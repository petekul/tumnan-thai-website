Tumnan Thai

Tumnan Thai is the legend of authentic Thai cuisine, inspired from a rich Thai heritage. All of our family recipes have their roots right from the heart of Bangkok itself.
For now over 20 years we have served the most Tantalizing Green and Red Curry, Aromatic Chicken Satay and our famous Phad Thai that will transport your taste buds straight to Thailand
Thai food is an exotic journey of the senses, created from a perfect blend of healthy herbs and spices to bring you into our world of flavour and harmony.
